Chevrolet Corvette C8
Main Info
The Chevrolet Corvette (C8) is the eighth generation of the Corvette sports car manufactured by American automobile manufacturer Chevrolet.
It is the first mid-engine Corvette since the model's introduction in 1953, differing from the traditional front-engine design.

The C8 was announced in April 2019, Production officially began on February 3, 2020
