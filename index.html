LivToAir
About Us
LivToAir is a solo developer company that makes addons for the Microsoft Flight Simulator 2020.
LivToAir has started with a different name in mid 2020, after the release of the Microsoft Flight Simulator 2020 and made liveries for the sim. In mid 2022, with the release of my first payware project, the XtremeAir Sbach 342, I changed the name to LivToAir.
The idea for the name "LivToAir" was to hide my journey as a developer inside the name. I started from designing liveries and later on started developing aircrafts. In short its Liveries To Aircrafts - LivToAir.

Main Developer: Yaniv Nadell
