XtremeAir Sbach 342
The XtremeAir Sbach 342 (XA42) is a German high performance two-seat aerobatic and touring monoplane designed by Philipp Steinbach with Albert Mylius and built by XtremeAir

It is the tandem version of the single-seater XtremeAir Sbach 300 (XA41) which was designed by the same team in in 2004. The XA42 received a type certificate from the European Aviation Safety Agency in March 2011. And in November 2012 it received its type certification through the FAA.

The production of this plane was stopped at the beginning of the year 2021
